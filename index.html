Discover Ilocos Sur

Explore scenic coastlines, rich history, and vibrant local crafts.

Featured: Ilocos Sur

Welcome to Ilocos Sur! A land of history, culture, and breathtaking beauty from the cobblestone streets of Vigan to the stunning coastlines and authentic local traditions, Ilocos Sur invites you to discover its timeless charm.

Tourist Spots & Adventures

1. Calle Crisologo

Calle Crisologo is a cobblestone street lined with ancestral houses that transports visitors back to Spanish-era Philippines. Enjoy a calesa ride and shop for handwoven goods along the way. The area is vibrant at dusk when lanterns and old lamps light the street.

#Vigan #CalleCrisologo #Heritage #CalesaRide #SpanishEra

2. Bantay Bell Tower

Bantay Bell Tower provides panoramic views of Vigan and the surrounding fields and coastline. Climb the tower for memorable photos and learn about the site's historical significance. Nearby are small shops selling local snacks and souvenirs.

#BantayBellTower #Viewpoint #History #Photography #IlocosSur

3.Santiago Ilocos Sur

Santiago, Ilocos Sur is best known for Santiago Cove, often called the ‘Boracay of the North’ because of its fine white sand and clear waters. It’s a perfect place to relax, swim, and enjoy the scenic view of the sea. The peaceful atmosphere, combined with local hospitality and nearby resorts, makes Santiago a must-visit beach destination in Ilocos Sur.

#SantiagoCove #IlocosSur #BoracayOfTheNorth #BeachVibes #WhiteSandParadise
